Traffic Signs & Signals

Understanding regulatory, preventive signs, and pavement markings

🔴 Regulatory Signs (R-series)

What are Regulatory Signs?

Red and white signs that show limitations and prohibitions. These tell you what you CANNOT do or MUST do. Examples: R-6 (No left turn), R-40 (Low beams required), R-48 (Loading zone).

⚠️ Preventive Signs (P-series)

What are Preventive Signs?

Yellow diamond signs that warn about dangers, hazards, or special conditions ahead. These tell you to BE CAREFUL. Examples: P-36 (Slippery surface), P-41 (Tunnel ahead), P-48 (Pedestrians), P-49 (School zone).

🛣️ Pavement Markings

What are Pavement Markings?

Lines and symbols painted on the road that regulate, warn, and guide traffic flow. Yellow lines (center), white lines (lanes), arrows (direction), and special markings. They work together with vertical signs!

💡 Green Light Checklist

Before proceeding on green, check: Arrows, Blocked intersection, Pedestrians, Police signals. Green doesn't mean GO - it means CHECK THEN GO!

🚗

Speed Limits

Maximum and minimum speed limits in Peru

💡 Quick Rule

Minimum speed = Half of maximum speed. Urban streets: 40 km/h max, School zones: 30 km/h max, Highways: 100 km/h max.

👥

Priority rules at intersections and special situations

▶️

Right of Way Video Lesson

Master priority rules at unregulated intersections, STOP signs, roundabouts, and pedestrian crossings

💡 Priority Hierarchy

1. Police signals (override everything) → 2. Traffic lights → 3. Traffic signs → 4. Right of way rules. Pedestrians ALWAYS have priority!

Driving Maneuvers

Overtaking, turning, lane changes, and parking rules

▶️

Maneuvers Video Lesson

Learn proper techniques for overtaking, lane changes, turning, and parking safely

⏱️ 3-4 minutes
💡 Golden Rules

Overtake on the LEFT. Signal FIRST, then change lanes. Urban turns: signal 20m before. Highway turns: signal 30m before. Signal the ENTIRE maneuver!

📄

Mandatory Documents

Essential documents you must carry while driving

💡 Key Point

You must carry 3 mandatory documents at all times: Identity Document, Valid SOAT, and Vehicle Identification Card. Virtual SOAT is accepted!

⚖️

Sanctions & Penalties

IF... THEN... rules for violations and consequences

⚠️ Most Severe Penalties

Alcohol/drugs + accident/refused test: Fine + Cancellation + PERMANENT ban. Driving without license: Fine + 3-year ban. 100 points in 24 months: License suspension.

🛡️ Safety Essentials
